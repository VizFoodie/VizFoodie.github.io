About Us


Course: University of Hawaii at Manoa - ICS 484 / ACM 484

Foodies is a group of ICS and ACM students at University of Hawaii at Manoa that's the dataset that we are using comes from food.com made by the user named Alvin at kaggle and it provides information about each recipe like cooking times, servings, ingredients, nutrition, and more. It contains 522,517 recipes from 312 different categories with 28 different attributes such as nutrition content(fat, saturated fat, cholesterol, sodium, etc.) and time(cook time, prep time, total time)
